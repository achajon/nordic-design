## Notes

- The hook line's `?!` is quoted, dramatized dialogue from a frustrated customer — not brand copy. `design-system.md` §9's "no exclamation points" guidance governs how *Nordic* talks; every line spoken as the brand (declaración, giro, promesa, cierre) stays exclamation-free, matching `como-agendar-citas`'s same rule. The hook is the one deliberate, explained exception.
- All body copy is set in Lost in South per the brand's Display/Signature role since every line here is a headline-weight statement, not paragraph body text — there is no Fira Sans Condensed body copy in this piece (no numerals either, so the digit-glyph bug in Lost in South never comes into play).
- "Forged for the close cut." is the brand's existing positioning line (`design-system.md` line 11), reused verbatim rather than inventing a new tagline for the close.
