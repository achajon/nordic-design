Forged for the close cut.

The visual system for Nordic Barber Shop — built on the discipline of a Viking edge: precise, angular, unhurried. Black is the steel, yellow is the sharpened line.

Est. lockup · Helmet mark + wordmark
01 — IDENTITY

The mark

One lockup, two ink colors. The Viking profile always leads the wordmark — never the reverse.

On Ink #101010Bone ink
On Bone #F7F4ECInk black

Usage rules

  • 01 Maintain clear space equal to the helmet's own height on all sides.
  • 02 Minimum digital width: 120px for the full lockup, 32px for the helmet mark alone.
  • 03 The helmet mark may run standalone as a favicon, stamp, or watermark — the wordmark may not.
  • 04 Always render in a single flat ink: bone or ink black. No gradients, no yellow fills on the mark itself.

Don't

Rotate · Stretch · Recolor · Add effects
02 — PALETTE

Ink & yellow

Three colors. Ink carries the room, yellow marks what matters, bone gives it air to breathe.

Nordic Yellow

The one accent. CTAs, tags, rules, price points. Use it like a blade — sparingly, with intent.

Bone

Primary text on ink, and the paper tone for print collateral.
03 — TYPOGRAPHY

Three voices

A struck headline, a hand-cut signature, and a calm working face for everything in between.

Lost in South
Headlines, section titles, prices. Always uppercase, always tight. The brand's own hand-cut face — now doing the structural work Anton used to.

Signature
Lost in South
Same font file as Display, set looser and without the forced uppercase — reserved for one accent phrase per layout: a promise, a call-out, a hand-finished detail. Never used for body copy or long lines.

Paragraphs, navigation, buttons, forms, schedules. Set in bone (white) on ink. Regular for reading, Semibold/Bold for labels and UI.

07 — FORMATS

Instagram sizes

Default to 4:5 for feed posts and 9:16 for Stories and Reels. Build the layout to the ratio — never stretch or crop the lockup to force a fit.

4:5
Feed post
1080 × 1350px
Default — matches the Social post frame component. Instagram's grid preview crops feed images to 3:4, so keep faces, logo, and copy inside the top 1080×1440.
1:1
Feed post, square
1080 × 1080px
Alternate only — use when a piece genuinely needs a centered, symmetrical crop.
9:16
Story / Reel
1080 × 1920px
Full-screen vertical — the only ratio with no bars or cropping. Keep logo and copy out of the top and bottom ~250px, reserved for Instagram's own UI.
08 — VOICE

How we talk

Confident, short, a little dry. We state the hours; we don't apologize for them.

Do

Lead with the specific: the cut, the hour, the price. Let the yellow underline do the emphasis, not extra words.

Fresh fade, guaranteed.
Don't

Don't stack adjectives or oversell. If the wordmark is loud, the copy stays quiet.
